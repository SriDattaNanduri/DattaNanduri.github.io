Awareness About Online Betting

What is Online Betting?

Online betting involves placing wagers on sports, games, or other events using internet-based platforms. While it may seem entertaining, it carries risks of addiction and financial losses.

Warning Signs of Online Betting Addiction

  • Spending more money than planned on betting
  • Feeling restless or irritable when not betting
  • Neglecting work, studies, or relationships
  • Borrowing money to continue betting
  • Hiding betting activities from family or friends

How to Avoid Online Betting Traps

  • Set strict time and money limits before betting
  • Do not chase losses by betting more
  • Keep yourself busy with hobbies and activities
  • Seek support from family and friends
  • Use website blockers to avoid betting sites

Where to Get Help

  • Talk to a counselor or mental health professional
  • Join support groups for gambling addiction
  • Contact helplines dedicated to gambling addiction
  • Educate yourself and others about risks of betting
