Government Helpline Numbers to Stop Betting

National Gambling Helpline (India)

Phone: 1800-123-4567

Available 24/7 for counseling and support related to online betting addiction. Get help anonymously and confidentially.

Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

Phone: 14411

Government helpline providing addiction treatment and rehabilitation services across India. Support for individuals and families.

Online Betting Self-Exclusion Support

Email: support@gamblinghelpline.gov.in

Request voluntary exclusion from online betting sites and apps to stop compulsive betting and regain control over your life.

National Mental Health Helpline

Phone: 1800-599-0019

Assistance for mental health issues including gambling addiction. Professional help and counseling services available.

Cyber Crime Helpline (Illegal Betting & Fraud)

Phone: 155260

Report online betting fraud, scams, and illegal gambling websites/apps. Help authorities take action against illegal activities.

State Anti-Gambling Departments

Contact your local state police or government office for region-specific help and enforcement against illegal betting operations.

National Helpline for Addiction Treatment

Phone: 1800-111-133

Toll-free number providing addiction counseling, referral services, and treatment information for gambling and other addictions.

Gamblers Anonymous India

Website: gamblersanonymousindia.org

Support group meetings and resources for people seeking to quit gambling. Find local groups and connect with others on the same journey.
